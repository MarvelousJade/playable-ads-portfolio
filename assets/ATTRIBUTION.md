# Asset attribution

## Symbol & coin art — Twemoji
- Files: `cherry`, `lemon`, `bell`, `star`, `gem`, `coin` (`svg/*.svg` sources and `*.png` rasterisations)
- Source: **Twemoji** — https://github.com/twitter/twemoji
- Copyright: © Twitter, Inc. and other contributors
- License: **CC-BY 4.0** — https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Graphics are unmodified Twemoji artwork, rasterised from the original SVGs to PNG
via `rasterize.js` and composited onto project-authored tile/cell frames at runtime.

All other artwork (tile frames, cabinet, wheel, foil, the jackpot 7), UI, animation
and audio were authored for this project.
